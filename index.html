Frequently Asked Questions

What is CreatorOS? +
CreatorOS is a next-generation operating system designed for creators, developers, and innovators. It combines powerful tools with an intuitive interface to help you bring your ideas to life in the digital realm.
How do I get started with CreatorOS? +
Getting started is easy! Simply download the installer from our official website, follow the installation wizard, and you'll be up and running in minutes. Check out our quick start guide in the documentation for a smooth onboarding experience.
What are the system requirements? +
CreatorOS requires a minimum of 8GB RAM, 20GB free disk space, and a modern processor (Intel i5/AMD Ryzen 5 or better). For optimal performance, we recommend 16GB RAM and an SSD. Full specifications are available in our documentation.
Is there a mobile version available? +
Yes! CreatorOS Mobile is available for both iOS and Android devices. The mobile companion app lets you access your projects, collaborate with team members, and manage your workflow on the go.
How can I report a bug or request a feature? +
We love hearing from our community! You can report bugs or request features through our GitHub repository, community forum, or by contacting our support team directly. Every submission is reviewed by our development team.
What support options are available? +
We offer multiple support channels including email support, live chat during business hours, an active Discord community, and comprehensive documentation. Premium users also get priority support and dedicated account managers.
